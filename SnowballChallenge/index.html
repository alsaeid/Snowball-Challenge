The state-owned green company

Our ambitions

Since the 1970s Norway has made billions of dollars extracting oil from the north sea. By doing this we have been contributing to climate change. We have a responsibility to now use our funds to turn the climate situation around. Enor is 70% owned by the Norwegian State and our goal is to produce environmentally friendly energy for the whole world.

We have our own production of solar panels and windmills. We are also invested and continue to invest companies that align with our mission.

Production in Norway is only contracted by the norwegian state. We also operate abroad, we have operations in every continent.
